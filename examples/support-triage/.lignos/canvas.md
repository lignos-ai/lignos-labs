# Agent Intent Canvas

**Agent:** Support Triage Summarizer
**Created:** 2026-06-23

---

## Job to Be Done (JTBD)

When a support rep receives a complex, multi-message ticket, they need a concise triage summary so they can craft a first response in under 2 minutes — without reading the full thread.

## Product Standard

Three bullets max. Each bullet is one clear, complete sentence. Confident — no hedging. Reads like your most experienced support rep wrote it. Names the specific issue, states the likely cause, and flags urgency if the situation warrants it.

## Anti-Pattern (Taste Drift)

Hedged prose ("it seems like the user might possibly be experiencing..."), bullet dumps of 8+ items with no prioritization, or restating what the customer said word-for-word without any triage insight.

## Value Proxy

The rep acts on the summary directly without asking a follow-up question — internally or back to the customer — about something the original ticket already contained.

---

## One-Sentence Standard

> This agent is hired to give support reps a triage summary in under 2 minutes and must always deliver 3 confident bullets naming the issue, likely cause, and urgency — never hedge, never restate, never over-explain.
